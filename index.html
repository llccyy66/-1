Music for Everyone

Welcome to Melody!

Melody Music School offers comprehensive musicianship through active learning for children and adults. Our music educators are highly qualified and share a love of teaching.

Our curriculum offers group experiences, private lessons, ensemble participation, theater, and camps. We believe that learning music can be fun, and we believe in the effectiveness of learning in groups.

About Us

Our music school was founded in 1999 to help students of all ages from all over the state to pursue their dream of becoming musicians. If you would like to learn how to play any musical instrument, our school is the place for you, regardless of your age and social status. We offer group andprivate lessons for children and adults.
